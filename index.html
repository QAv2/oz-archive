The Oz Archive is a 3D museum with 6 exhibit corridors branching from a central hex atrium. Exhibits include: Disclosure Files, Qualia Algebra, Intel Console, Suppressed Physics, YouTube Pipeline, and Iceberg Index. Two hidden portals exist: one in the floor (WorldView) and one in the ceiling (device geometry). Use WASD to move, E to interact with exhibits, and T to speak with the AI curator.
